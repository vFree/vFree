vFree by vFree

A web based veterans services management system

vFree is a system that is being developed to meet the specific needs of the Freedom House in Kent, Ohio, and the general needs of any homeless shelter or VA Per-Diem program. The Freedom House is a small facility that provides a safe environment for homeless veterans to establish healthy and productive lifestyles. To facilitate the Freedom House's mission, we are developing vFree to securely track information about all clients and potential clients the Freedom House comes into contact with, from initial contact well into the after-care and follow up phases. The some features we will include are:

  • record phone screen data
  • record needs assessments
  • goal setting
  • track progress towards goals
  • maintain counseling notes
  • maintain activity records (meetings, classes, volunteer activity etc.)
  • client calendars
  • management reporting
  • low level document management functionality
This project will be developed by volunteers and anyone who would like to help out is welcome to do so in any capacity that they feel comfortable.
